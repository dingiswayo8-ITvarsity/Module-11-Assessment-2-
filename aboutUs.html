How it all started

Hi, my name is Claire Woods and I am a mother to two beautiful kids. I started this blog because I love food and I have always loved trying out new things.I have travelled aroud the world visiting different restaurants and eating new foods.

I learned to cherish food and recipes that remind me of family, friends and life events, this space allows me to share my battered, overstuffed recipe binder and the stories behind them.

I plan to share my experience with you and hope my blog will bring a differeve into your life. It may be food, a restuarant or a recipe, I want you to experience it too and lets make our taste buds smile. I hope you enjoy it and visit again soon!
