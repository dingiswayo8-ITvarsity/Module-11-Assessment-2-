Beach House

Camps Bay, Cape Town

Using locally sourced and ethically-farmed ingredients that sing with flavour is the key focus when it comes to the food served at Beach House. Their menu will adapt regularly to ensure our produce is fresh and seasonal, but there will always be a few staple favourites year-round.

Rykaart’s

Stellenbosch, Cape Town.

Rykaart’s | Stellenbosch is a family owned restaurant run by Alex and Ella Rykaart. Situated in the heart of Stellenbosch town with quintessential views overlooking beautiful old buildings and oak trees, Rykaart’s serves simple, contemporary food made using locally sourced, seasonal ingredients.

Last updated 12 April
Christelle’s Bistro

Swellendam, Western Cape

This cosy French Bistro in the heart of Swellendam, the 3rd oldest town in South Africa, is run by owners, chef Josia Conradie and his charming French wife, Christelle. They serve authentic French food with a South African flair.
